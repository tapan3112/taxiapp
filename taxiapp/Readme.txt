1. After Installing the Application you have to give permission for Location and Storage.
2. Map will open.
3. On tapping "search here" you will ask "Enter Pickup" and "Where to?" places.
For Example :
"Casa da Música" as initial point and "Aeroporto Francisco Sá Carneiro" as final point.
4.You will get the complete route details on map and Price on message Popup.
5.Message popup will display inner and outer distance with its Price rate and total distance with total Price.
